How to play

Advance through all seven levels of the crawsword before you run out of guesses.

Each level contains a single crossword clue to solve. The clues become harder as you advance throught the levels.

If you check and your answer is wrong, a guess will be used up and the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the answer.

The letter A is in the answer and in the correct spot.

The letter O is in the answer but in the wrong spot.

The letter T is not in the answer in any spot.
